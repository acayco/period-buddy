Project Description

Feminine care has come a long way to meet the different needs of menstruating people. It’s not just pads and tampons that are out there anymore! Although there are plenty of options in the sea now, it’s so easy to feel overwhelmed with these new choices. Take our quiz to find the best product for your menstrual experience.
